Welcome to Our Site!

We are a merchant export organization with core competencies in Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients, Herbal extracts, Nutraceuticals ingredients and Cosmetic ingredients. We have been providing our services to various companies in the pharmaceutical industry for more than 28 years.A tradition of professionalism has been very well established at Shubham Pharmachem in the past almost three decades, scaling new horizons with tremendous capability and appropriate infrastructure. A quintessential and a well-leveraged network of sales interface is complemented with well-equipped and organised representation through our offices in Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Bangalore, Lagos (Nigeria), Accra (Ghana) AND Khartoum (North Sudan).With proper dimensional pursuits of the Pharmaceutical industry, exhaustive and extensive research has enabled us to seamlessly integrate products and logistics for comprehensive distribution to our counterparts. Our repertoire is the key to perfect combinations of coordination, implementations and customer care.

Semi Finished Formulations(PREMIX)

With an aim to cater to global FDF manufacturers, Prime Pharma brings its technological and cost advantages to them by providing Semi Finished Formulations (Premix) with an easy single step process.

Nutraceuticals and OTC Healthcare.

As people worldwide turned to natural alternatives for a healthier life, Prime Pharma has introduced a unique range of nutraceutical products. In 2009 Prime started an OTC Healthcare Division with focus on Quality and Affordable Healthcare Products which are usually sold Over The Counter. Prime Pharma has carefully selected products and support plants which manufacture products of the highest quality.

Regulatory

Prime Pharma’s focus has always been to deliver high quality products and an integral part of this is the Technical Documentation and support that it provides to its global partners.

Prime has invested earnestly in this sphere and a full fledged team of experienced regulatory experts are preparing Dossiers in various formats including CTD and ACTD guidelines.

Prime has a large database of Dossiers in various global formats. The Dossier team is continuously adding to this database, with an average addition of 50 dossiers annually
